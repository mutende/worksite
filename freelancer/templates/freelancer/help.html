If there are task available, you are allowed only to bid once and incase you have more than 3 pending task you cannnot bid for anyother task until your workload is done. This is to reduce the greed for money and to make sure you do quality work

The client with the task that you bidded will review your profile and onces they are satisfied they can assign you a task where you are required to finish with the stated time limit or else you will be fined and rated low

Once you have completed a task, submit it on time and if not reassined you will be rated and payed the full amount. Incase of delay in payments, contact the admin via the platform.
