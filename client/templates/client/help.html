Help Page

Online Worksite is a platform where you as a client is linked with free mobile xperinced workers which are reffered to as Freelancers. These freelancers will help to relieve some work load at small fee upon payments. We are glad that you have trust in our platform and believe that you will have a great experience. Incase of any concerns please do not hesitate to talk to us.

For signup you are required to provide some information that will identify you. The phone you proved needs to follow 254711111111 format and the phone is recommended to be your personal phone, this will help to easien payments.

Once signup is successful you can log in with correct credentials and explore the system.

Incase there is misconduct your account can be deactivated as a warning or completely blocked, you should take note of that

Once inside the system Navigate into task zone and you will take control of posting task process.

First the task details are provided and then proceed to payments, Unpaid task are not visible for bidding

Make sure you have enough amount in your MPESA account to carry out payments

When a freelancer submits a task make sure you recieve and rate them as to how they have done your work. If you continuously dont rate them your account can be DEACTIVATED as a warning

If the submitted work does not meet the expected standards you are allowed to reassign them only once. To avoid reassigning, provide clear descriptions for how you need your tasks to be handled.

In comment sections you are allowed even to ask questions.
