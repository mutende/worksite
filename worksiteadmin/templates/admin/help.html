GoodLuck

Super admin account is created during system development, incase a new admin is added, there is a specific procedure for creating their accoount. The supper admin understands the procedure of creating a new account.

Once the account has been created, you can log in your credentials, upate your profile and start monitoring users activities. All the admin actions are recorded as logs in the database.

If the contract on the platform is terminated, your account can be deleted.

Clients, These are people who post tasks to the platform.

When their accounts are created, they are active by default, if they misbehave on the platform, the admin has the power to deactivate their accounts.

Client Related Reports

  1. Client Records

    This report should contain a list of clients who have registered successfully

  2. Tasks

    This report should contain a list of task posted and are paid for, meaning jobs that can be bidded.

  3. Reassigned Tasks

    This report should contain a list of tasks, that were done poorly and reassined to freelancers

  4. Job Payment Summery

    This report should contain a list of payment summery of tasks from the client

Freelancers, These are people who bid for tasks and work on them then submit complete task back to the clients.

When their accounts are created, you need to activate for them to start placing bids on different task. When they are assigned a task the clients rate them as to how they have done the task . A freelancer who is continually rated low might speak a poor quality staff,its is recommended to deactivate their accounts.

Freelacer Related Reports

  1. Freelancer Records

    This report should contain a list of freelancers who have registered successfully

  2. Bids

    This report should contain a list of bids placed by specific freelancers and whether they were assinged or not

  3. Payment Summery

    This report should contain a list of payment summery for freelancers after attending to some task.
